what has been on my mind for months?

hey there lovely! happy third monthsary, prince! bigla ka siguro na isang mock website ang ginawa ko for our monthsary noh? honestly, i was planning to give this on the sixth monthsary pero naisip ko, baka sobra na kong busy nun kaya eto na siya! i hope that you like it kasi marami ka pang matatanggap na katulad nito in the near or far future heh
so ayun, third monthsary na natin at for the past few weeks, i am really sorry na naging ganito ang situation ko pero thankful ako na naiintindihan mo siya kahit alam ko nakakafrustrate rin minsan. thank you for staying with me kahit alam kong mahirap ako pakisamahan, madaldal and sobrang sensitive and emotional at many times. thank you for staying talaga baby ah. yun lang kasi ang gusto ko for us, tumagal tayo and tayo lang forever.
and then there are days or nights na we talk about the future like meeting the parents, walking down the aisle and more and ako lang ba toh or ngayon lang ako sobrang excited when i talk about it na may halong nervousness kasi the future can get a bit uncertain? alam ko naman na we can do this together hehe.
